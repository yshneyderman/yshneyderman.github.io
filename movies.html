Favorite Movies / TV


As someone with experience in the industry and having seen many movies, when considering a movie or show for this list, I tend to consider the following: cinematography, plot, writing, acting, budget, history, genre, uniqueness. Generally a movie or tv show will need to do well in all categories to make the list, but in some cases I give special consideration for exemplary performance in certain categories. The list contains movies in several languages, but most are in English except where otherwise stated.


In no particular order, the movies and tv shows that I felt were above a certain threshold to make them worthy of this list.
